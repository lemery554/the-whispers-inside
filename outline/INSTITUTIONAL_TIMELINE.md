## Progression rules

- No system is hacked and no single conspiracy takes control.
- Changed workers preserve technical competence while redefining the desired outcome.
- During Phase 3, the similar wording has a visible administrative path: hospital discharge language informs state health guidance, which is reused by schools, employers, police de-escalation materials, and local news. The language can benefit changed people before those institutions are captured.
- Each phase retains traces of the last; the shift is gradual enough to rationalize.
- Public claims should be technically true whenever possible. Manipulation comes from omission, emphasis, repetition, and comforting language.
- Brooke notices alignment across systems before she understands why it is happening.
