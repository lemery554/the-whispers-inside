# Chapter Architecture

**Status:** Complete working architecture for review. Prose drafting remains gated.

**Target:** Prologue plus 29 numbered chapters, approximately 80,000 words. Brooke is the sole narrative viewpoint. The final journal image is the only controlled exception. The climax and ending compress into a single night (Day 26): siege, escape, the engineered car pickup, and the journal reveal.

## Prologue: The Color

- **Day:** 1
- **Purpose / setting:** Brooke reviews and saves early public footage at her editing desk as hospital awareness breaks nationally.
- **Emotional goal:** Detached curiosity to disturbed attention.
- **Conflict:** She wants clean evidence, but every source contradicts another.
- **Reveal:** Patients violently reject the red medicine, then appear to recover.
- **Hidden clues / payoff:** Synchronized vomiting, comforting recovery language, and Brooke's habit of preserving footage pay into the institutional shift and Anna's later study.
- **Character movement:** Brooke decides this beginning is worth recording.
- **Tension:** 3/10
- **Found media:** Emergency clips, hospital footage, dispatch audio, comments.
- **Estimated words:** 2,500
- **Continuity:** Close third with Brooke; she sees all public material included.
- **Question answered / created:** What is entering public awareness? / Why does recovery feel wrong?

- **Purpose / setting:** The engineered rescue — Anna's car appears as Brooke runs, and they drive away.
- **Emotional goal:** Terror to dangerous relief.
