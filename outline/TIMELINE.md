## Continuity constraints

- Everyone who completes the purge eventually changes; no cure or recovery occurs.
- Mark's communication degrades, then stops. His fate stays unknown.
- School remains open through institutional drift.
- Anna makes two confirmed school-day house entries (TWI-009): the first (investigation) is Day 18; the second (journal theft) is Day 19.
- Anna's two in-person meetings with Brooke precede the climax: a casual dog-park encounter on Day 11 (no identity claimed) and the diner meeting on Day 23 (biological-mother reveal-to-Brooke).
- The narrative concludes on the night of Day 26 (siege → escape → engineered car pickup → journal reveal, all one night). Days 27–30 of institutional drift (Phases 5→6) continue off-page and are not narrated.
- The changed-police "reunion" beat is cut: no officers arrive on the escape night.
- Brooke does not become sick, but the story never defines her as immune.
- Touch does not transmit the illness, but no character knows the real route and exposure scenes must preserve that uncertainty.
