# Rejected Ideas

These directions are explicitly out of canon and must not be reintroduced without direct approval.

- Brooke is immune, genetically special, chosen, or part of a bloodline mythology.
- Anna searches for Brooke's real biological mother or uses Brooke as bait.
- Brooke has a biological brother connected to an older outbreak.
- Anyone has years of prior research into this month-long outbreak.
- The cause is an ancient entity, alien presence, government experiment, fully explained parasite, or other solved mythology.
- A full cure or full recovery appears in this novel.
- The changed receive a formal category or monster name.
- Society collapses immediately into a conventional apocalypse.
- Anna becomes aggressive, knowingly evil, or a traditional villain.
- The ending relies only on a green-eye reveal.
- Changed people lose all memories or become mindless.
- Brooke has a romantic subplot.

Rejected ideas are retained here for continuity defense, not future brainstorming.
