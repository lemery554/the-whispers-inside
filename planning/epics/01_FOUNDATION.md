# Epic 1: Foundation and Novel Bible

**Milestone:** M1

**Priority:** P0

**Target:** 2 to 4 weeks

**Depends on:** Nothing

**Blocks:** All prose drafting

## Outcome

Create a reliable author-facing model of the people, month, places, clues, and behavioral rules. The model may preserve mystery for readers, but it cannot contain accidental contradictions for the author.

## Included tasks

TWI-001 through TWI-023 in `planning/BACKLOG.md`.

## Deliverables

- Full character dossiers
- Locked or deliberately unresolved canon decisions
- Day-by-day calendar
- Clue ledger
- Bennett house floor plan
- Town guide and map
- Hospital research notes
- Infection behavior matrix
- News/civic continuity calendar
- Foundation continuity report

## Definition of done

All P0 tasks are accepted, all P1 omissions have an explicit rationale, and the documents agree on dates, places, access, knowledge, and behavioral progression.
