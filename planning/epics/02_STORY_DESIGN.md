# Epic 2: Story Design

**Milestone:** M2

**Priority:** P0

**Depends on:** Epic 1

## Outcome

Turn the foundation into a complete 30-to-40-chapter dramatic plan in which every major reveal is earned and every chapter changes the story.

TWI-101 through TWI-110.

The chapter outline projects 85,000 to 100,000 words, every chapter has purpose/conflict/reveal/clue/growth fields, the Anna reveal passes a reread audit, and drafting receives explicit authorization.
