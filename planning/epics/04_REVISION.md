# Epic 4: Cooling and Revision

**Milestone:** M4

**Priority:** P0 after Draft One

**Depends on:** TWI-205

## Outcome

Create distance, diagnose the manuscript honestly, complete multiple focused rewrites, test it with qualified readers, and pursue professional editing appropriate to the publication goal.

## Included tasks

TWI-301 through TWI-307.

## Beta-reader success questions

- Where were you bored?
- Where were you confused?
- Who did you trust, and when did that change?
- When did you suspect Anna?
- Did the ending feel both surprising and earned?
