# Town and Setting

## Working location

Ashcroft, Connecticut is the current working town and state, but both require validation before publication.

## Desired character

A close-knit but not tiny American suburb with a major local hospital, one high school, woods, a familiar grocery store, local news, back roads, cul-de-sacs, and access to larger-city panic.

## Key locations

- Bennett home: Brooke's editing setup, Claire's room, upstairs hallway, kitchen, living room, basement or garage, and Mark's gun safe
- Hospital emergency department
- High school
- Grocery store
- Neighborhood streets and back roads
- Woods
- Final escape road

## World rule

Collapse is gradual. Schools, shops, restaurants, offices, neighborhood groups, utilities, and media continue operating while their social meaning becomes subtly wrong.
