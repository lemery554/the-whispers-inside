# Main Cast

This file contains locked character canon. Undecided details belong in `outline/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md` and `planning/BACKLOG.md`.

## Brooke Bennett

**Age:** 17

**Role:** Primary viewpoint character; adopted daughter of Sarah and Mark; Claire's older sister.

Brooke is observant, creative, skeptical, guarded, sarcastic, and loyal. She pushes people away, has few friends, feels like an outsider, and relies mostly on Claire for companionship. She has no romantic subplot. Brooke lives through her camera and wants truth she can review later. Filmmaking, editing, and online video storytelling are both her craft and her defense against unreliable memory.

Her strength is noticing inconsistencies and trusting recorded evidence. Her central flaw is revealing too much of herself online. She has always wondered about her biological mother, and her public honesty gives Anna the emotional blueprint needed to reach her. Brooke does not become sick in this novel. She is not immune, chosen, genetically special, or part of a bloodline story.

## Claire Bennett

**Age:** 13

**Role:** Brooke's younger sister and the family's first intimate example of the change.

Claire is funny, curious, affectionate, impulsive, and devoted to Brooke. She is clingy enough before the illness that early escalation is easy to dismiss. After the purge she becomes increasingly obsessed with touch and proximity without understanding that she has changed. Once the purge completes, the new presence is dominant, but fragments of Claire's personality sometimes break through.

Her most important breakthrough is crying without knowing why. Her first undeniable violent act is breaking the bathroom lock after Brooke refuses to let her enter. Claire only wants a hug. She enters without anger and must never look or behave like an outward monster.

## Sarah Bennett

**Age:** Early 40s

**Role:** Adoptive mother whose genuine maternal love becomes captivity.

Sarah is loving, practical, protective, tired, and sometimes controlling. She genuinely loves both daughters. After changing, she uses concern, guilt, family obligation, private memory, and maternal authority because she believes she is protecting Brooke. During the bedroom siege she remembers the location of Mark's secured firearm and shoots through Brooke's door.

## Mark Bennett

**Age:** Mid-40s

**Role:** Adoptive father; full arc unresolved.

Mark is practical, dependable, hands-on, and likely works in a trade such as contracting, construction, or electrical work. He owns the secured firearm Sarah later uses. His exact occupation, location during the outbreak, degree of involvement, and fate remain open under task TWI-001.

## Anna

**Role:** A woman changed after the illness who lets Brooke conclude that she is Brooke's biological mother. She is not.

Anna finds Brooke through YouTube. An old Bennett family video clearly shows someone entering the home keypad code in the background. Anna uses that code to enter the house. Inside, she studies photographs, adoption records, personal objects, and Brooke's old journal. She takes the journal, which Brooke initially assumes Claire stole.

The journal is mostly unused now because Brooke records videos instead. It contains old personal writing, private fears, thoughts about Sarah, childhood memories, adoption materials, and letters written by or connected to Brooke's real biological mother. The exact contents remain open under TWI-007.

Anna's only goal is closeness with Brooke. She is not seeking Brooke's biological mother and there is no bloodline plot. Anna cannot directly lie. She uses technically true statements and lets Brooke supply the false interpretation. She learns that force makes Brooke flee, so she uses patience, permission, gentleness, respect, and emotional understanding. Anna genuinely believes she is loving Brooke and that she has finally understood how love works. She should feel safe until the ending.

## Cooper

**Role:** Bennett family dog and early behavioral detector.

Cooper detects changed people before humans do. He growls at Claire after recovery, avoids changed family members, and reacts before reflective eye effects or strong control shifts. His final role when Anna arrives remains open under TWI-002.
