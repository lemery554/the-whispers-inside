# Main Cast

## Brooke Bennett

Seventeen, adopted by Sarah and Mark, and Claire's older sister. Brooke is observant, creative, skeptical, guarded, sarcastic, loyal, thoughtful, and sometimes anxious. She wants a future in filmmaking or visual storytelling. She records ordinary life because she believes memory quietly rewrites events.

Her strength is noticing inconsistencies and trusting recorded evidence. Her weakness is that her candid videos reveal her fears, loneliness, adoption wound, and desire to be found. Her footage helps survivors recognize patterns, but gives Anna a psychological blueprint. Brooke is not biologically special.

## Claire Bennett

Thirteen. Funny, curious, affectionate, impulsive, and devoted to Brooke before the illness. Her preexisting affection makes her early change difficult to identify. After recovery she needs escalating proximity, enters Brooke's room, watches her sleep, repeats comforting phrases, hears whispers, and eventually uses force. Fragments of Claire may resist, leaving genuine ambiguity.

## Sarah Bennett

Early forties. Loving, practical, exhausted, protective, warm, sometimes controlling, and sometimes dismissive of Brooke's fears. After recovery, genuine maternal love becomes possession. She uses guilt, family obligation, private memories, and authority. She believes keeping Brooke home is protection.

## Mark Bennett

Mid-forties. Dependable, practical, hands-on, and always repairing something. His exact trade, outbreak role, and fate remain open. He owns a secured firearm whose location Sarah remembers during the bedroom siege.

## Anna

A recovered woman who allows Brooke to conclude that she is Brooke's biological mother. She is not. Anna studies Brooke's uploads and materials taken from the Bennett home. She learns that force makes Brooke flee, so she respects boundaries, accepts rejection, asks permission, listens, waits, and rarely needs to lie directly. Her sole objective is closeness with Brooke. She should remain calm, patient, protective, emotionally intelligent, and believable.

## Cooper

The Bennett family dog. Cooper detects recovered people before humans do and can warn through avoidance, growling, barking, or pulling Brooke away. His final status and reaction to Anna remain open.
